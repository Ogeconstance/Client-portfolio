Non-Humanitarian Project

Voters' Registry
Extraction and Fraud
Analysis

Multiple reports of erroneous/fraudulently registered voters
in the national registry

Methodology:

Web scraping techniques (selenium) were implemented to extract publicly available voters' records from the national registry. The images of the voters were then transformed and convolution neural network was then used to identify the under aged voters. Duplicate names and voter IDs were identified and EDA was carried to check the distribution of these errors across the country, in order to discover any patterns.
